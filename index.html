About 14 Empire

14 Empire started on 15th June of 2017 to bring change in the restaurant industry in Patna. 14 Empire is totally a contemporary concept bringing music and food together and making it more happening place in Patna. With 14 Empire we have brought a change in the restaurant industry in Bihar where we have live music which happens on weekends and we call different artists to perform. Our cuisine is totally fusion to blend with the taste of India.
